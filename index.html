Raw Artizan

Modern Artist & Photographer

Artist Statement

Covers portraits, photojournalism, and abstract photos. His photojournalism looks at modern political standpoints in history, such as Covid-19 and 9/11. This work captures the faces of the masses during volatile demonstrations in the name of freedom of expression. His work is socially relevant, modern, and impactfulâ€”provoking thought, intent, and realization of self-expression.

Biography

Raw Artizan is a modern artist and photographer based in Central London. He graduated from Nescot College of Technology with a degree in photography and briefly studied Fashion Promotion and Imaging at UCA in Epsom in 2014.

Abstract

Raw Artizan explores natural and organic shapes, patterns, and colors. His keen eye for dramatic, unorthodox compositions and vivid colors is inspired by the unseen beauty of nature and his deep love for the earth.

Inspiration & Influences

Growing up in urban West London in the 90s, Raw Artizan was inspired by his community. His influences include Andy Warhol (including his collaboration with Basquiat), Picasso, David Bowie, Esther Anderson, Rankin, Banksy, Bob Marley, and the Hip Hop genre.
